What does Delta Chi Value?

There are four principles outlined in the Delta Chi Preamble that accurately represent the Fraternity's values: Friendship, Character, Justice, and Education. These are further expressed through Delta Chi's Eleven Basic Expectations:

Expectations

I. I will strive for academic achievement and practice academic integrity.

II. I will respect the dignity and worth of all persons. I will not physically, mentally, psychologically or sexually abuse or haze any human being.

III. I will protect the health and safety of all human beings.

IV. I will respect my property and the property of others; therefore, I will neither abuse nor tolerate the abuse of property.

V. I will meet my financial obligations in a timely manner.

VI. I will neither use nor support the use of illegal drugs; I will neither abuse nor support the abuse of alcohol.

VII. I will acknowledge that a clean and attractive environment is essential to both physical and mental health; therefore, I will do all in my power to see that the chapter property is safe, properly cleaned and maintained.

VIII. I will know and understand the ideals expressed in my fraternity Ritual and will incorporate them into my daily life. IX. I will exercise compassion and understanding in dealing with all persons.

X. I will sustain my commitment to and involvement with our fraternity throughout my lifetime.

XI. I will challenge all my fraternity members to abide by these fraternity obligations and will confront those who violate them.
